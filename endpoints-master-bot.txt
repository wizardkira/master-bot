MASTER BOT - ENDPOINTS (resumo legivel)

CORE EXECUCAO
1) RPC_URL_PRIMARY / SECONDARY / TERTIARY (Solana JSON-RPC)
   Retorno: result/error (slot, balance, account info, send tx)
   Potencial: base do bot (leitura on-chain + execucao)

2) RPC_WSS_URL / PUMPFUN_PROGRAM_WSS_URL
   Retorno: subscription id + eventos de conta/programa
   Potencial: reacao em tempo real

3) wss://pumpportal.fun/api/data
   Retorno: novos tokens (mint, symbol, marketCapSol, etc.)
   Potencial: discovery rapido

4) https://pumpportal.fun/api/trade-local
   Retorno: transacao serializada (buy/sell)
   Potencial: execucao rapida pump/raydium

PUMP.FUN MARKET DATA
5) https://swap-api.pump.fun/v1/coins/{mint}/market-activity?program=pump
   Retorno: 5m/1h com volumeUSD, numBuys, numSells, etc.
   Potencial: filtros de tracao

6) https://swap-api.pump.fun/v2/coins/{mint}/trades?... 
   Retorno: trades (type, amountSol, timestamp)
   Potencial: detectar whale e dump

7) https://frontend-api-v3.pump.fun/coins/{mint}
   Retorno: dados do token (usd_market_cap, name, symbol)
   Potencial: filtro por mcap

8) https://frontend-api-v3.pump.fun/coins-v2/{mint}
   Retorno: creator, socials, reserves, timestamps
   Potencial: due diligence

9) https://frontend-api-v3.pump.fun/coins/{mint}/holders?...
   Retorno: holders e percentuais
   Potencial: risco de concentracao/rug

10) Endpoints de listagem:
    - /coins/recommended
    - /coins/currently-live
    - /coins/trending-search-v2
    - /coins?sort=...
    Retorno: arrays de tokens
    Potencial: scanners new/movers/traction

11) https://frontend-api-v3.pump.fun/callout/list/{id}?...
    Retorno: callouts com coin/token/mint
    Potencial: sinais sociais curados

12) https://advanced-api-v2.pump.fun/coins/kolscan
    https://advanced-api-v2.pump.fun/coins/graduated
    Retorno: coins (coinMint, marketCap, creationTime)
    Potencial: universo alternativo de entrada
